The general form of an \gap{exponential} \gap{function} is 
\begin{tcolorbox}[center,width=4in,colback=white,]
    \begin{center}
        \large
        $f(x) = A \, b^x$
    \end{center}
    where
    \begin{itemize}[nosep]
        \item $A$ is the \gap{initial} \gap{value} 
        \item $b$ is the \gap{base} 
    \end{itemize}
\end{tcolorbox}
