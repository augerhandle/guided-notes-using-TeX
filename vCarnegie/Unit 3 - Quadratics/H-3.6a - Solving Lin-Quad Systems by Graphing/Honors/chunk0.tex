    \end{center}

\begin{tcolorbox}[center,width=4.75in,colback=white]
    A \gap{linear}-\gap{quadratic} \gap{system} 
    consists of 
    \begin{itemize}[nosep]
        \item one linear equation (a \gap{line}) 
        \item one quadratic equation (a \gap{parabola}).
    \end{itemize}
\end{tcolorbox}

Linear-quadratic systems might have 
\gap{two}, \gap{one}, or \gap{no} solutions.
