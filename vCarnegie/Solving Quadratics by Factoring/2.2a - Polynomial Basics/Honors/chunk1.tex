\section{Classifying Polynomials}

Polynomials are also {\bfseries\itshape classified} by \gap[d]{degree}:
\begin{center}
    \large\renewcommand{\arraystretch}{1.5}
    \begin{tabular}{|l|l|l|}
        \hline
        \gap{constant} 
            & a polynomial of degree \gap{0}
            & $9$ \\
        \hline
        \gap{linear} 
            & a polynomial of degree \gap{1}
            & $2 - 3x$ \\
        \hline
        \gap{quadratic} 
            & a polynomial of degree \gap{2}
            & $6x - 2x^2 +9$ \\ 
        \hline
        \gap{cubic} 
            & a polynomial of degree \gap{3} 
            & $5x^2 + x - 27 + 8x^3$ \\
        \hline
    \end{tabular}    
\end{center}


{
Polynomials \gap{never} have variables that
\begin{itemize}
    \item have \gap{negative} exponents
    \item are in the \gap{denominator}
    \item have \gap{non-integer} exponents
    \item are under a \gap{radical}
\end{itemize}
}
