\section{Relative Maxima and Minima}

Maxima and minima are like \gap{hills} and \gap{valleys}.
\begin{itemize}[]
    \item A \gap{relative} \gap{maximum} is the \gap{point} at the \gap{top} of a hill.
    \item A \gap{relative} \gap{minimum} is the \gap{point} at the \gap{bottom} of a valley.
\end{itemize}
