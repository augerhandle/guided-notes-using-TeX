\section*{Problem 2 ---  Prescription Medicine Spending}
This table shows spending on prescription medicine in the US 
from 2015 to 2022.
% https://www.statista.com/statistics/184914/prescription-drug-expenditures-in-the-us-since-1960/
\begin{center}
    \small
    \renewcommand{\arraystretch}{1.25}
    \begin{tabular}{c|c|c}
        \toprule
        year 
            & 
                \begin{tabular}{c}
                    $x$\\
                    {\tiny\itshape years since 2015}\\
                \end{tabular}
            & 
            \begin{tabular}{c}
                $y$\\
                {\tiny\itshape spending  (billions of \$)}\\
            \end{tabular}
            \\
        \midrule 
        2015 & \gap{0}  & 312.2 \\
        2016 & \gap{1}  & 313.3\\
        2017 & \gap{2}  & 315.7 \\

        % 2019 & \gap{4}  & 335.7 \\

        2021 & \gap{6}  & 374.5 \\
        2022 & \gap{7}  & 405.9 \\
        \bottomrule
    \end{tabular}
\end{center}
Use \myDesmos to do \myEmph{linear} and \myEmph{quadratic} regressions on these data.
\begin{itemize}[nosep]
    \item Fill in the second column ({\itshape years since 2015}).
    \item Enter the $x$ and $y$ data into a \myDesmos $\mathtt{x_1}$--$\mathtt{y_1}$ table.
    \item Do a \myEmph{linear} and \myEmph{quadratic} regressions in \myDesmos{}.
    \item Write the regression equations, and find the values of $r^2$.
\end{itemize}

\noindent

Explain your reasoning.\\[0.65\onelineskip]
