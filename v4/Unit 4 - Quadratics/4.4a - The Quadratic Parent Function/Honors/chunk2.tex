\section{Attributes of the Quadratic Parent Function}

\subsection{Vertex}
The {\bfseries\itshape vertex} is the \gap{turning} \gap{point} 
where the curve heads back in the other direction.

\subsection{Axis of Symmetry}
The {\bfseries\itshape axis of symmetry} ({\itshape AOS})
is the \gap{vertical} \gap{line} 
that divides the parabola into two \gap{equal} halves.

\subsection{Domain and Range} 
The {\bfseries\itshape domain} is all the \gap{$x$-values} on the curve.
The {\bfseries\itshape range} is all the \gap{$y$-values}.

\subsection{Maximum or minimum?}
The vertex is a {\bfseries\itshape maximum} if it is at the \gap{top} of the curve.
The vertex is a {\bfseries\itshape minimum} if it is at the \gap{bottom}.

\subsection{Opens up or down?}
The parabola {\bfseries\itshape opens up} if the ``arms'' extend \gap{upward}.
The parabola {\bfseries\itshape opens down} if they extend \gap{downward}.
