\section{The Augmented Matrix}

An \gap{augmented matrix} is a simpler way to write systems of equations.
It has variable \gap{coefficients} in the first several columns 
and the \gap{constant} terms in the last column.

\hfil
{
  \sysdelim..\systeme{
    2x+3y+4z=1,
    5x+6y+7z=2,
    8x+9y+10z=3
}
{
  \LARGE
  $\Longleftrightarrow$
}
{
  \(
    \begin{bmatrix}[ccc|c]
      2 & 3 & 4 & 1\\
      5 & 6 & 7 & 2\\
      8 & 9 & 10 & 3
    \end{bmatrix}
  \)
}
}
{\scshape Dimensions:} \gap{$3\times4$}
\hfil
