\section{Elimination using Matrices}

In this lesson,
you will learn how matrices can be used to
solve systems of equations by elimination.
We will do it with an example.

\myWideProblemWithContent[
    Solve this system of equations.
]
{
    \begin{center}
        % \sysdelim..
        \systeme{
            2x+3y=4,
            5x-7y=-19
        }
    \end{center}
    \[
        \begin{bmatrix}[cc|c]
          2 & 3 & 4\\
          5 & -7 & -19
        \end{bmatrix}
    \]
    \[
        \begin{bmatrix}[cc|c]
          10 & 15 & 20\\
          -10 & 14 & 38
        \end{bmatrix}
    \]
    \[
        \begin{bmatrix}[cc|c]
          10 & 15 & 20\\
          0 & 29 & 58
        \end{bmatrix}
    \]
    \[
        \begin{bmatrix}[cc|c]
          10 & 15 & 20\\
          0 & 1 & 2
        \end{bmatrix}
    \]
    \[ 0x + 1y = 2 \]
    \[ y = 2 \]
    \[ 2x + 3y = 4 \] 
    \[ 2x + 3(2) = 4 \] 
    \[ 2x + 6 = 4 \] 
    \[ 2x = -2 \] 
    \[ x = -1 \] 
}[\Large]
