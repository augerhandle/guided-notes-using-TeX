\section{Matrices}

These are \gap{matrices}:
\(
\begin{bmatrix}
  1 & 2 & -1 & 5\\
  3 & 4 & -3 & 3
\end{bmatrix}
\)
%
\hfil 
%
\(
\begin{bmatrix}
  1 & 0 & 0 \\
  0 & 1 & 0 \\
  0 & 0 & 1
\end{bmatrix}
\)
%
\hfil 
%
\(
\begin{bmatrix}
  1 & 2 \\
  0 & 3 
\end{bmatrix}
\)

Matrices can represent systems of equations.
For example, this system
\begin{center}
    \large
    \sysdelim..
    \systeme{
        7x-3y=24,
        5x-2y=16
    }
\end{center}

can be represented by this \gap{augmented} matrix
{
    \large
    \vspace{2\baselineskip}
    \[
    \begin{bmatrix}[cc|c]
    7 & -3 & 24\\
    5 & -2 & 16
    \end{bmatrix}
    \]
}
