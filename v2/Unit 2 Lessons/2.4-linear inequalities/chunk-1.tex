
\section*{Inequalities and Number Lines}

There are two ways to write the solutions to algebraic inequalities:
\begin{itemize}[itemsep=0in]
    \item as {\bfseries\itshape simple inequalities}
    \item as {\bfseries\itshape number line graphs}
\end{itemize}
They are related as follows.

\begin{center}
\begin{tcolorbox}[width=4in]
    \begin{center}\Large
        \begin{tabular}{c|l}
            $x > a $    &  \quad\,\,    $\underset{a}{\circ}\kern-0.45em\longrightarrow$   \renewcommand{\arraystretch}{2}   \\
            $x\geq a$   &  \quad\,\,    $\underset{a}{\bullet}\kern-0.5em\longrightarrow$   \\
            $x<a$       &  \hskip .15em $\longleftarrow\kern-0.45em\underset{a}{\circ}$  \\
            $x\leq a$   &  \hskip .15em $\longleftarrow\kern-0.5em\underset{a}{\bullet}$
        \end{tabular}
        \renewcommand{\arraystretch}{1.0}
    \end{center}
    \vskip 1em
    The arrows point the same direction as the inequalities
    only if the variable is {\bfseries\itshape on the left}!
\end{tcolorbox}
\end{center}


\begin{taggedblock}{on-level}

\begin{myExample}{Write these inequalities as a number line graphs:}
    \LARGE
    \begin{minipage}{0.49\textwidth}
        \begin{flalign*}
            & x \leq 4 & \\ \\
            & x > -3 & 
        \end{flalign*}
    \end{minipage}
    \begin{minipage}{0.49\textwidth}
        \begin{flalign*}
            & x < 2 & \\ \\
            & x \geq 5 & 
        \end{flalign*}
    \end{minipage}
    \vspace{1em}
\end{myExample}

\end{taggedblock}
