\begin{myConceptSteps}{To multiply a binomial and a binomial\dots}
    \myStep{multiply}{
        Multiply each of the terms in the first binomial
        into each of the terms in the second binomial. 
        That will give you $2 \times 2=4$ terms.
    }
    \myStep{combine}{Combine like terms.}
\end{myConceptSteps}

You learned this as FOIL in Algebra 1.
