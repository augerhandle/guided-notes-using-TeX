
\section*{Multiplying complex numbers}

Multiplying complex numbers is just like multiplying polynomials:
{\bfseries\itshape distribute} the first one into the second one.

\begin{myConceptSteps}{
    To multiply two complex numbers\dots
    }
    \myStep{distribute}{
        Distribute the \gap{first} complex number into the \gap{second} one.
    }
    \myStep{replace $i^2$}{
        Replace $i^2$ by $-1$.
    }
    \myStep{combine}{
        Combine the \gap{real} and \gap{imaginary} parts.
    }
\end{myConceptSteps}




\begin{taggedblock}{on-level}
\myBlankExample{2in}{
    Multiply these complex numbers:
    \[ 
        (4 - 2i) (3 + 5i)
    \]
}
\end{taggedblock}
