\section{Inverse Variation}

\begin{minipage}{0.71\textwidth}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item \gap{inverse} variation describes a relationship between two variables.
        \item They are {\bfseries\itshape inversely} \gap{proporational}.
        \item When one variable \gap{increases}, the other variable \gap{decreases}.
        \item When one variable \gap{decreases}, the other variable \gap{increases}.
        \item Their \gap{product} is {\bfseries\itshape constant}.
        \item This is a \gap{rational} function.
    \end{itemize}
    
\end{minipage}
