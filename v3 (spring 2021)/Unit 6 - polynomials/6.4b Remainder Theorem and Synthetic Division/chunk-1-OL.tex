\section{The Remainder Theorem}

For any polynomial $f(x)$,
the \gap{Remainder} Theorem says:
%
\begin{myCenteredBox}[width=4.75in]
    To evaluate $f(a)$, calculate the \gap{remainder} of 
    \large
    \begin{equation*}
        \frac{f(x)}{x-a}
    \end{equation*}
\end{myCenteredBox}
