
We can look at systems
\gap{algebraically} or \gap{geometrically}:

\vspace{1\baselineskip}
\noindent
\begin{minipage}{0.45\textwidth}
    \begin{center}
        \large
        \begin{align*}
            x - y &= 0 \\
            x + 2y &= 6 
            % 2y = -x + 6
            % y = -0.5x + 3
        \end{align*}
    \end{center}
\end{minipage}
