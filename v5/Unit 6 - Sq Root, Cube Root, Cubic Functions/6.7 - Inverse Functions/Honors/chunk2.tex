\section{Inverse Functions}

\begin{minipage}{0.49\textwidth}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item \gap{Inverse} functions are \gap{maps} that tell you 
            how to \gap{go} \gap{back} where you started.    
        \item You can ``follow the arrows'' starting 
            on the $y$-axis and going back to the $x$-axis.\
        \item Notice that we're \gap{switching} $x$ and $y$. 
            Instead of $x\mapsto y$, we're doing 
            $y\mapsto x$.
    \end{itemize}
\end{minipage}
