\section{Making sure the $(x,y)$ data are in sensible units}

\begin{tcolorbox}[center,width=5.5in]
    \small
    Sometimes the units on $x$ or $y$ are ``mixed''. 
    To use the values in \myDesmos or a \myTi calculator, 
    you must first convert to a \gap{single} \gap{unit}. 
    For example,
    \begin{itemize}[nosep]
        \item convert \myEmph{foot--inch} data to \myEmph{inches} (5'2'' $\rightarrow$ 62'')
            \item convert \myEmph{minute--second} data to \myEmph{seconds} (3:10.2 $\rightarrow$ 190.2 seconds)
    \end{itemize}
\end{tcolorbox}
